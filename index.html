The Details

It is IMPORTANT you know the details, most of the details are designed to keep all costing reasonable and not to waste.

  1. The service is booked a week in advance by e- transfer only.
  2. You must pay for your meal service starting on Thursaday at 5:00 pm and closing by Monday at 10 AM, Thus reserving your Meal Package for the following week.
  3. We reserve on a first come first served basis after. So please order early.
  4. We do have a limit on the number of spaces, when we are full we will send out and email informing you of any single meals we may have left and that the weekly capacity has been reached.
  5. Delivery is on Sunday and is $10 cash. This is paid directly to the driver or by envelope in the mailbox. We respect our driver very much.
